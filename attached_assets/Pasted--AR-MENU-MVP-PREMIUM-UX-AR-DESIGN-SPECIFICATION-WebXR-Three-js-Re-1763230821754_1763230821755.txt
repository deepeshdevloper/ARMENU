# 0. PRODUCT PURPOSE

This MVP allows customers to scan a simple restaurant QR and instantly experience a **high-fidelity AR food preview**.
The AR menu provides:

* Hyper-visual, Snapchat-inspired category selection (Lens Rings).
* Creative dish listing.
* Zero-lag WebXR AR placement of food items on a detected table.
* Strict rule: **No horizontal surface = No AR dish model.**
* Premium Gen-Z friendly interface with micro-animations and neon effects.

No payments.
No user accounts.
No restaurant admin panel.
Only scanning, viewing categories, choosing a dish, and placing it in AR.

# 3. AR EXPERIENCE SPECIFICATION (CORE OF MVP)

---

## **3.1 Entering AR Mode**

### Motion

* Fade UI
* Camera feed appears
* Three.js renderer activates
* Reticle appears only when hit-test finds a surface

---

## **3.2 Plane Detection Rules**

This is the MOST important MVP rule:

### **The dish model MUST NOT appear until a valid horizontal surface (table/plate) is detected.**

### Conditions for ‚Äúvalid plane‚Äù:

* Plane normal must be facing upward
* Angle tolerance: max tilt ‚â§ 20¬∞
* Minimum hit-test stability:

  * Hit-test must be consistent for 300‚Äì600ms
* If no valid plane for 6 seconds ‚Üí show ‚ÄúNo Table‚Äù guidance screen

---

## **3.3 Reticle Design**

* Circular thin glowing ring
* Dot in center
* Neon color matches dish category (consistent theme)
* Pulses lightly when plane becomes stable
* Reticle has two states:

### **Reticle States**

1. **Searching for surface (No plane)**

   * Reticle hidden
   * ‚ÄúMove your device slowly‚Ä¶‚Äù text appears

2. **Valid plane found**

   * Reticle visible
   * Glow intensifies
   * Small bounce animation indicating placement possible

---

## **3.4 Placement Interaction**

### How placement works:

* User taps anywhere while reticle is visible
* Dish model spawns anchored to plane
* Model scale applied smoothly from 0 ‚Üí full scale (0.4 sec ease-out animation)
* Info Overlay appears after placement

---

## **3.5 AR Dish Interaction Rules**

### Scaling (Pinch Gesture)

* Pinch in/out to shrink/grow
* Limit scale to 0.5√ó ‚Üí 2.0√ó

### Rotation (Two-Finger Rotate)

* Smooth rotation proportional to twist angle
* Slight easing applied to make movement feel natural

### Position Lock

* Once placed, dish stays anchored
* To move: user must remove & re-place (simple for MVP)

---

## **3.6 Info Overlay UI**

Small floating panel at top or bottom:

* Dish name
* Calories
* Ingredients
* ‚ÄúReposition Dish‚Äù button
* ‚ÄúExit AR‚Äù button

Overlay automatically dims after 3 seconds of inactivity.

---

## **3.7 Exiting AR**

* Fade out camera feed
* Fade in Dish List screen
* Dispose AR session gracefully

# 6. PERFORMANCE GUIDELINES

---

## **6.1 AR Performance**

* Models optimally < 5 MB
* Use compressed glTF
* Texture resolution minimal to avoid lag
* Keep shadows simple or baked
* Keep materials PBR but lightweight

---

## **6.2 Loading Optimization**

* Preload low-res model preview
* Load full model only when entering AR
